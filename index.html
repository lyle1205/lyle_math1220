Course Topics: equations, functions and graphs, with emphasis on linear, quadratic, polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions. The course emphasizes algebraic problem solving and graphical representation of functions.

Grading: Your course grade will be determined from written homework, quizzes, in-class activity, ALEKS, two unit exams.

Homework: Weekly homework is due on the posted due date and submit through Canvas as PDF files. Please submit neat, organized work.
