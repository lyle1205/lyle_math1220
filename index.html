Course Topics: equations, functions and graphs, with emphasis on linear, quadratic, polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions. The course emphasizes algebraic problem solving and graphical representation of functions.


Prerequisite: Math 1215XYZ or appropriate placement. See Prerequisite review. Extra Practice: Exponent and Radicals, Factoring, Fraction and Rational Expression, Equation and Inequalities,


Textbook and Online HW: College Algebra with CoRequisite Support, 1st edition, Julie Miller and Donna Gerken. Course materials and online homework are accessed through ALEKS in Canvas.


UNM Canvas: Course materials, communication, and grades will be posted on Canvas.


Grade:Your course grade will be determined from written homework, quizzes, in-class activity, ALEKS, two unit exams.

Homework: Weekly homework is due on the posted due date and submit through Canvas as PDF files. Please submit neat, organized work. We will have quiz the same day homework is due or the day after so be prepared. Aleks Assignments must complete on your own time so check the due date on Canvas or Aleks.


Resources: Please use office hours and campus support resources if you need help. I want to hear your questions and help you succeed. Below is course syllabus make sure to read it througly for your success if you have any question do not hesitate to ask.
